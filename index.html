Fighter at the Studio

"from a great fighter to a Professional Great Fighter".

Luis Baboon Palomino

The Fighter Studio has the honor to help building the image of one of the best fighter at CFA (Luis Baboon Palomino). We studied his character and his skills in order to come up with a professional image.

Palominos' logo design was based on the "baboon" monkey as they call him. It was designed using the scariest and horrifying part of this specie, the wide open mouth and sharped fans.

We also came out with a different marketing idea of building a fighter machine to give that steel, iron and unbeatable look. The results were awesome and his fans were very satisfied.
